Project Overview

Oak is a premium camping equipment brand that needed a responsive e-commerce website to showcase their products and provide a seamless shopping experience across all devices. The project focused on creating an intuitive interface that caters to camping enthusiasts who value quality equipment and detailed product information.

Key Pain Points & Solutions

Mobile Experience

With 85% of traffic coming from mobile devices, the existing platform wasn't optimized for smaller screens. Complex navigation, slow loading times, and difficult checkout processes led to high bounce rates.

Oak Solution

Implemented a mobile-first responsive design with touch-friendly interfaces, optimized images, and streamlined checkout process. Created a progressive web app experience for faster loading.

Product Discovery

Users struggled to find specific camping gear due to poor categorization and filtering. Lack of detailed product information and comparisons made purchase decisions difficult.

Oak Solution

Developed intuitive category navigation with advanced filtering by activity, season, weight, and price. Added comprehensive product pages with 360¬∞ views, detailed specs, and customer reviews.
