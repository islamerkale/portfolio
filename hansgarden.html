Project Overview

HANS' GARDEN is a premium olive oil brand based in the USA, offering high-quality, organic olive oils sourced from family-owned groves. The project involved creating a modern e-commerce website that reflects the brand's premium quality while providing an intuitive shopping experience.

Key Challenges & Solutions

Premium Digital Experience

Creating a digital experience that conveys the premium quality of HANS' GARDEN olive oils was challenging. The website needed to reflect the brand's elegance and quality while remaining user-friendly and accessible.

Sophisticated Visual Design

Implemented a sophisticated color palette inspired by olive groves, elegant typography, and high-quality product photography. Created visual hierarchy that emphasizes product quality while maintaining usability.

Subscription Implementation

Implementing a flexible subscription model that encourages repeat purchases while providing customers with customization options. The challenge was balancing simplicity with flexibility.

Flexible Subscription System

Developed an intuitive subscription system with clear options for frequency, product selection, and customization. Implemented visual indicators of savings and benefits to encourage sign-ups.

Design Process

1

Research & Discovery

Conducted market research on premium food and beverage e-commerce sites. Analyzed competitor strategies and identified opportunities for differentiation. Interviewed potential customers to understand shopping behaviors and expectations for premium food products.

"Premium food shoppers value transparency about sourcing, high-quality product imagery, and clear information about taste profiles and usage suggestions."

2

Information Architecture

Structured the platform to balance product discovery with brand storytelling. Key sections include Products, Our Story, Production Process, Recipes, and Subscription options. Focused on creating intuitive navigation that guides users from discovery to purchase.

3

Wireframing & Prototyping

Developed low-fidelity wireframes focusing on clean, elegant layouts that showcase products while maintaining easy navigation. Progressed to high-fidelity prototypes with brand visual elements, focusing on creating a premium aesthetic.

4

Visual Design & Testing

Implemented sophisticated visual design reflecting HANS' GARDEN's brand identity with elegant typography, strategic color usage, and high-quality imagery. Conducted multiple rounds of user testing to refine the shopping experience.

Information Architecture

The information architecture was designed to provide intuitive navigation and easy access to products, brand story, and subscription options for premium food shoppers.

Wireframes

Initial wireframes focused on establishing the core layout, content hierarchy, and user flow before moving to detailed visual design.

"The wireframing phase helped optimize the product presentation and subscription flow, resulting in a 42% increase in subscription sign-ups post-launch."
