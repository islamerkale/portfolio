Project Overview

DC Ticket is a mobile application designed to simplify the process of finding, contesting, and paying parking tickets in Washington DC. The app provides a user-friendly interface that helps citizens manage their parking violations efficiently.

Key Problems & Solutions

Fragmented Information

Users had to visit multiple websites and use different systems to find, contest, and pay parking tickets, creating confusion and frustration.

Unified Dashboard

Created a single dashboard that shows all tickets, their statuses, and provides unified actions for contesting, paying, or viewing details.

Complex Contest Process

The current process to contest a ticket was confusing, required multiple steps, and users often abandoned the process midway.

Simplified Contest Flow

Implemented a step-by-step guided contest process with clear instructions, progress tracking, and easy evidence upload.

User Research

Research Methodology

To understand the pain points and needs of users dealing with parking tickets, I conducted:

  • • 15 in-depth user interviews with DC residents
  • • Survey of 100+ respondents about their ticket experiences
  • • Competitive analysis of existing ticket payment solutions
  • • Heuristic evaluation of current government portals

"Most users expressed frustration with the current system, citing confusing navigation, lack of mobile optimization, and unclear instructions as major pain points."

Usability Testing

Testing Methodology

We conducted moderated usability tests with 12 participants representing our target demographics to identify areas for improvement.

Key Findings

  • • Payment confirmation needed improvement
  • • Evidence upload was confusing
  • • Navigation required simplification
