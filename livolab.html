Key Challenges & Solutions

Service Tier Differentiation

LivoLab offers three distinct service tiers (Gold, Diamond, VIP) with different features, pricing, and target audiences. The challenge was to clearly differentiate these tiers while maintaining a cohesive brand identity.

Visual Package Cards

Created distinct visual cards for each service tier with clear feature lists, pricing, and highlighted benefits. Implemented a package quiz to guide users to the right service tier based on their needs and experience level.

Building Instructor Credibility

As an online platform, establishing trust and credibility for the instructor was crucial. Students needed to feel confident in Hacer's qualifications and teaching approach before committing to classes.

Comprehensive Instructor Profile

Developed an extensive "About Me" section showcasing Hacer's background, certifications, teaching philosophy, and specialization areas. Included a visual timeline of her training and certifications to build credibility.

Design Process

1

Research & Discovery

Conducted user interviews with potential Pilates students in Turkey to understand their needs, concerns, and decision-making process when choosing online fitness programs. Analyzed competitor websites and pricing models.

"Turkish students value instructor credibility, clear pricing, and flexibility. They need to quickly understand the differences between service tiers and feel confident in the instructor's qualifications."

2

Information Architecture

Structured the website to prioritize service discovery with clear pathways to package selection. Key sections include Packages, About Me, Training, Pilates Information, and LIVO Philosophy. Created user flows for each service tier.

3

Wireframing & Prototyping

Developed low-fidelity wireframes focusing on service tier differentiation and mobile optimization. Created interactive prototypes to test the package selection process and booking flow with potential users.

4

Visual Design & Testing

Implemented a clean, wellness-focused visual design reflecting the premium nature of Pilates training. Used calming colors, elegant typography, and authentic imagery.
