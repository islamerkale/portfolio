Key Pain Points & Solutions

Food Waste

Restaurants struggle with significant food waste due to spoiled ingredients and poor portion control. Without proper tracking systems, perishable items often expire before use, and inconsistent portion sizes lead to unnecessary overuse of ingredients.

This results in thousands of dollars lost monthly and contributes to environmental concerns in the food industry.

IN+ Solution

IN+ implements smart expiration tracking and automated portion control recommendations. The system alerts staff when ingredients are approaching expiration and suggests menu items to prioritize.

Visual recipe management ensures consistent portion sizes, reducing ingredient overuse while maintaining quality standards.

Cost Management

Restaurant managers lack real-time visibility into inventory costs and fluctuations. Without accurate tracking, they can't identify cost-saving opportunities or detect unusual spending patterns.

This leads to budget overruns, inability to optimize menu pricing, and missed opportunities for bulk purchasing discounts.

IN+ Solution

Real-time cost tracking and predictive analytics provide immediate insights into spending patterns. The system automatically calculates ingredient costs per menu item and suggests optimal pricing strategies.

Automated supplier price comparison ensures restaurants always get the best deals on ingredients.

Multi-Platform Chaos

Modern restaurants operate across multiple delivery platforms (Uber Eats, DoorDash, Deliveroo), but lack a centralized system to manage inventory across all channels. This leads to overselling, stockouts, and manual reconciliation errors.

Staff waste hours each day manually updating inventory counts across different platforms.

IN+ Solution

Seamless integration with all major delivery platforms automatically syncs inventory in real-time. When an item sells out on one platform, it's automatically updated across all channels.

The centralized dashboard provides a unified view of sales performance and inventory levels across all platforms, eliminating manual work and preventing overselling.

Inefficient Ordering

Restaurants struggle with inventory ordering - either overstocking (leading to waste) or understocking (leading to stockouts). Without data-driven insights, ordering decisions are based on guesswork rather than actual demand patterns.

This results in emergency orders with premium pricing and lost sales during peak hours.

IN+ Solution

AI-driven predictive ordering analyzes historical sales data, seasonal trends, and local events to generate optimal purchase suggestions. The system accounts for lead times and minimum order quantities from suppliers.

Automated reorder points ensure restaurants never run out of critical ingredients while minimizing excess inventory.

Design Process

1

Research & Discovery

Conducted comprehensive user research with restaurant owners, chefs, and inventory managers. Analyzed existing inventory management solutions and identified key pain points through surveys and interviews.

"We discovered that 78% of restaurants still use manual inventory tracking methods, leading to significant revenue loss and operational inefficiencies."

2

Information Architecture

Structured the platform around core modules: Dashboard, Inventory Management, Recipe & Costing, Multi-Platform Integration, and Analytics. Created user flows for each primary task.

Information architecture diagrams showing module relationships and user flows

3

Wireframing & Prototyping

Developed low-fidelity wireframes to establish layout and user flow. Progressed to high-fidelity prototypes with interactive elements for usability testing and stakeholder feedback.

"The wireframing phase helped us identify critical usability issues early, saving development time and ensuring a smoother user experience."

4

Visual Design & Testing

Implemented a clean, professional design system with dark theme for prolonged use. Conducted multiple rounds of user testing to refine interactions and improve usability.

Final design mockups showing dashboard interface, mobile app, and key features
